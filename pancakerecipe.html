we would like to start with what you need to have:

ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cup of Flour
  • 3 and 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 and 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter

Now that we have all ingredients, we can start cooking Pancakes.

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the
    • Flour
    • baking power
    • salt
    • sugar
  2. after you make it well sifted, in the center add:
    • milk
    • egg
    • melted butter
  3. Mix it until smooth.
  4. Heat lightly oiled frying pans over medium high heat
  5. Pour or scoop 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan. make it sure to have a good circle
  6. Flip it over when the base of the pancake is brown, and it is ready when both sides are brown.
  7. serve hot and use some syrup and condensed milk as well
